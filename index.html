moneypacket.org

Digital envelopes for real bitcoins, no trusted third parties.
Offline
Preferred units:

A money packet is a file which contains bitcoins, like a digital envelope containing money.

Like any other file, money packets can be shared over email or Dropbox or backed up for storage.

This site lets you to create money packets and claim their funds.

Recipients don't need a bitcoin wallet to receive funds.
All funds are password protected by a password you create.
The sender can re-claim funds until the receiver claims them.
This website is free, open source, and runs only on your computer, not a third party server.
There is no account sign up whatsoever.
